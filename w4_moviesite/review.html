REVIEW

John Doe Febuary 2, 2022

OMG, My kids and I love this movie so much! This film has all the same things you've come to love about the PAW Patrol TV series with a new action-packed adventure and new-found friends.

Suited up and ready for an all-new adventure, your favorite group of heroic pups, led by a boy named Ryder, are back in Paw Patrol: The Movie!

This family fun film is sure to capture the hearts of new and original PAW Patrol fans.

Trivia

  • This is the second time Paw Patrol has shown how one of the members joined the team. In the show, a segment called "Pups Get A Rubble" shows how Rubble joined the team, in the movie Ryder tells the other pups how he found Chase
  • For the role of Ryder, over 1000 young actors auditioned from all over the USA and Canada, before Albertan high school student Will Brisbin got the part.
  • This is the first PAW Patrol film to be released theatrically as the previous three PAW Patrol films: "Mighty Pups", "Ready Race Rescue", and "Jet to the Rescue" were direct to DVD TV movies.
